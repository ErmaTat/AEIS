PRIVACY POLICY

NLCGA complies with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR, in collecting and processing your data.
The online platform includes a robust networking function, which allows all registered participants to find and connect with each other through the platform.
Basic information that you share during registration will be used to create your profile on the platform: this does not include any contact information.
You will have the ability to edit your profile if you join the online Application platform.
NLCGA collects and processes data for the purposes of fulfilling your registration order, analyzing how this site performs and is used, and marketing for future related NLCGA events.
All users maintain the right of erasure and can withdraw their consent at any time. Please see NLCGA privacy policy for additional information.
By engaging with exhibition stands or sponsor content, users are consenting to exhibiting and sponsoring companies having access to the personal data that is public in their personal profile.
You may also be contacted, through the platform only, by other registrants including attendees, speakers, and poster presenters looking to connect with you.

3.1 Full payment of due on submission of the booking form to the organizers. The participant however, acknowledges that sending the booking form or contract is a demonstration of ability and intent to make full payment.
