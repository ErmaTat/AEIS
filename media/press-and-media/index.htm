MEDIA/ASSOCIATION PARTNER

The World Utilities Congress provides a platform for media and association partners, to gain direct exposure to high-level officials throughout the global power and water utilities industry. Our partnership packages are tailored to suit various objectives by offering you the opportunity to:

  • Meet face-to-face with energy government, policymakers and industry leaders
  • Gain exposure across print event collateral and the official event website
  • Promote and distribute your publication during the 3 event days
