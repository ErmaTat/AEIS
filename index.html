About the Conference

15th Annual NLCGA Conference
& LPG Industry Exhibition.

The NLCGA Conference & Exhibition brings together key stakeholders, policymakers, investors, and experts in Nigeria’s LPG sector to discuss progress, challenges, and opportunities within the industry. This landmark 15th edition aims to foster innovation, encourage collaboration, and promote sustainable energy solutions across the nation.

  • Industry Networking Sessions
  • Policy & Regulatory Panels
  • Exhibition of LPG Solutions
  • Technical Presentations
  • Workshops & Masterclasses
  • Investment Opportunities
