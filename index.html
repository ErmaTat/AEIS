About The Summit

The Inaugural Gathering
of Africa’s Energy Future

AEIS 2025 is the first international platform dedicated exclusively to accelerating energy investment across Africa. Held in New York, this groundbreaking summit is expected to bring together 500+ investors, policymakers, and innovators committed to shaping the continent’s energy transformation.
