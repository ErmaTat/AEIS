Bridging Continents: Connecting Investors Worldwide with Africa's Energy Potential

The Africa Energy Investment Summit is a high-level continental platform, endorsed by regional institutions and development partners, designed to accelerate investment across Africa’s energy value chain. It is where bold ambitions meet global capital, policy meets innovation, and deals that power millions are born.

Each year, the summit convenes African governments, global investors, energy developers, DFIs, and multilateral agencies to unlock transformative energy partnerships. From traditional hydrocarbons to renewables, transmission, and off-grid solutions, the AEIS fosters conversations and commitments that shape the future of African energy.

With initiatives like Mission 300, the summit is at the heart of Africa’s push for universal access, energy security, and sustainable growth. It’s not just a conference—it’s a catalyst for change, forging strategic pathways toward a connected, electrified, and empowered continent.
